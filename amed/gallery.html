System Archive.

A chronological record of our most complex sartorial commissions. From technical outerwear to architectural evening-wear, each entry represents a successful collaboration between artisan and client.

The Curation Process

The Archive is not merely a gallery. It is a study of evolution. We document our commissions to track the refinement of our silhouettes and the innovative application of new textiles. Each piece shown here was built to a specific technical brief, addressing the unique postural and aesthetic requirements of its wearer.

Material Heritage

Many of the garments featured in the Archive utilise deadstock fabrics from world-class mills, or high-performance technical textiles developed for endurance. By preserving these records, we allow new clients to draw inspiration from our technical history.
