SEO Performance

Search engine visibility is a long-term asset. We build SEO strategies that improve your organic search performance in a structured, sustainable way — not with shortcuts that stop working when algorithms change.

What we offer

What's included

Technical SEO audit and implementation
On-page optimisation (meta, structure, internal links)
Keyword research and content strategy
Local SEO for businesses serving specific geographic areas
Backlink acquisition and authority building
Monthly ranking, traffic, and performance reports
How it works

Our process

01

Audit

Full technical and on-page SEO review. We look at site structure, crawlability, page speed, meta data, and existing rankings to understand where you actually stand.

02

Priority Plan

Ranked list of fixes and opportunities by expected impact. Not everything gets done at once — we work through what moves the needle first.

03

Implementation

On-page fixes, technical corrections, content creation, and link building. We can work directly in your CMS or hand off to your team.

04

Monthly Reporting

Track rankings, organic traffic, and progress against targets. Results are contextualised — no vanity metrics, no unexplained spikes.

Who this is for

Is this right for you?

SEO Performance is the right fit for businesses that rely on organic search traffic and want to grow it, local businesses trying to rank for location-based searches in a competitive area, and companies launching new websites or content programs and wanting to build search visibility from day one. It's also right for businesses that previously invested in SEO but didn't see results and want to understand why.
