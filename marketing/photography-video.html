Photography & Video

Visual content shapes how your brand is perceived before a single word is read. We produce professional photography and video that gives your business a look worth noticing, across your website, social media, and marketing materials.

What we offer

What's included

Brand and lifestyle photography
Corporate headshots and team photography
Product photography
Brand video and promotional content
Social media short-form video content
Video editing and post-production
How it works

Our process

01

Creative Brief

Understand the output requirements, intended use, visual style, and brand guidelines. We align on what "good" looks like before any production is planned.

02

Pre-Production

Shot list, location scouting, scheduling, and logistics. Every hour on set is planned before we get there.

03

Production

Professional shoot, on-location or in studio, with direction to get the shots we planned. We work efficiently and don't waste your time.

04

Delivery

Edited and colour-graded content delivered in the formats and dimensions you need, ready for your channels without additional processing.

Who this is for

Is this right for you?

Photography and Video is the right fit for businesses that need professional visual assets for their website and marketing but are currently using stock imagery or inconsistent phone photography, brands launching new products or services that require dedicated visual content, and companies whose current visual identity varies across different channels. If your business looks different on your website versus your LinkedIn versus your socials, that inconsistency has a cost.
