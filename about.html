About Simmico

We started from a straightforward observation: most businesses manage their IT and their marketing as completely separate problems, handled by separate vendors with no coordination between them. We built Simmico to fix that.

Our story

The gap between IT and Marketing costs businesses real money

When a business builds a new website, the development team focuses on code and infrastructure. The marketing team focuses on messaging and conversion. In most cases, these two groups don't share a brief, don't agree on priorities, and finish with a product that satisfies neither side.

That's a structural problem, not a personnel one. And it repeats across every project — cybersecurity decisions made without considering how marketing tools handle data, automation built without understanding the campaigns it's supposed to support, social media managed by someone who has no visibility into the CRM it's supposed to feed.

Simmico operates across both domains. Our IT and marketing capability is under the same roof, which means projects are briefed once, coordinated properly, and delivered without the gaps that come from managing two separate vendor relationships.

Our mission

"Handle the technology and marketing of growing businesses as a single, coordinated function."

Not two separate services bolted together. One capability, one team, one relationship.

What we stand for

Our values

Clarity over complexity

We explain what we're doing and why. No jargon, no black boxes. You should always know what's happening with your systems and campaigns.

Scope discipline

We define what we're delivering before we start. Scope creep is usually a briefing failure. We take the brief seriously.

Practical over impressive

The best solution is the one that solves the problem reliably, not the most technically interesting one. We build things that work and are maintainable.

Long-term thinking

We're not interested in short-term fixes that create bigger problems later. Every recommendation is made with the next two years in mind, not just the next sprint.

Our team

People who know both sides

The Simmico team combines technical and creative disciplines. We don't outsource to generalists when depth is needed — the people on your project are the people who know the domain.
