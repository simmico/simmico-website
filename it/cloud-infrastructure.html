Cloud & Infrastructure

Reliable infrastructure is the foundation everything else runs on. We design, deploy, and manage cloud environments that are secure, cost-efficient, and ready to scale when your business needs it.

What we offer

What's included

Cloud environment setup (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
On-premise to cloud migration with minimal downtime
Server provisioning and ongoing management
Database setup, backup strategy, and disaster recovery
Cost optimization and resource rightsizing
Infrastructure monitoring with alerting and incident response
How it works

Our process

01

Assessment

Review your current infrastructure, workload patterns, compliance requirements, and budget. We document what exists before recommending anything.

02

Architecture Planning

Design a cloud environment that matches your actual needs — not over-engineered, not under-resourced. You review and approve before we build.

03

Migration & Deployment

Migrate existing workloads with a plan that minimises downtime. New environments are provisioned with security and cost controls in place from day one.

04

Ongoing Management

Monitor performance, manage costs, apply patches, and handle incidents. Monthly reporting covers usage, spend, and any notable events.

Who this is for

Is this right for you?

Cloud and Infrastructure is the right fit for businesses outgrowing shared hosting or struggling with unreliable on-premise hardware, companies moving to the cloud for the first time and wanting it done properly, and startups needing a production-grade setup from day one. It's also for existing cloud users who aren't confident their environment is secure, optimised, or well-monitored.
